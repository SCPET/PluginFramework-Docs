##  `RoundEndReason` : `Enum`
Determines the reason the round ended. Used in the `Summary` struct.

### `0` AllEscape
All players escaped.

### `1` AllDied
All players died.

### `2` Forced
The round was ended via admin command.

### `3` Automatic
The round was ended via the automatic round restart timer.

### `4` Unknown
Unknown round end reason.
